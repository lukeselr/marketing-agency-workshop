## Big-spend triggers

- Meta Business Verification (DUNS) only required for >$50/day spend. Recommend ABN extract first (free).
- Google Ads Standard developer-token tier only needed past 15,000 ops/day. Basic tier is free + sufficient for most.
- LinkedIn Sales Nav ($99/mo) optional — unlocks deeper targeting + scraping. Skip until needed.

## Safety rails

- Phase 8 launches all campaigns **PAUSED** by default.
- Phase 9 kill-rule monitor pauses any campaign hitting $50 spend with zero conversions.
- No campaign goes live without your explicit "approve and activate" via chat or Telegram.
